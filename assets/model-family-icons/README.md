- Most icons come from [Lobe Icons](https://github.com/lobehub/lobe-icons), pinned to `@lobehub/icons-static-svg` version `1.94.0`. They are distributed under the included MIT license.
- `sakana-fugu.png` is the fish mark cropped from the official [Sakana AI logo](https://sakana.ai/sakana-logo.png).
